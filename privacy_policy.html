Privacy Policy

App: SMART Water Level Control BD  |  Version: 1.0.0  |  Last Updated: May 29, 2026

Developer: Smart IoT Interface  |  Contact: smartiotinterface@gmail.com

This app is designed to control and monitor water tanks using an ESP32 IoT device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address and display name (via Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • Device Data: Water level readings, pump status, sensor data from your ESP32 device
  • Usage Data: App settings, schedules, automation rules, scenes you create
  • Location: Not collected
  • Payment Info: Not collected

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your water tank status on the dashboard
  • To send push notifications (pump alerts, level warnings)
  • To sync your device settings across sessions
  • To enable device sharing with other users
  • To improve app stability via crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (Google LLC, USA) and protected by Firebase security rules. Data is accessible only by authenticated users who own or have been granted access to a device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, database, crash reporting, push notifications
  • Google Sign-In: Optional login method

These services have their own privacy policies. Firebase: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide the service (e.g., Firebase services).

6. Device Sharing Feature

If you use the "Share Device" feature, the email address of the person you share with will be stored in the database to grant them access. You can remove shared users at any time from the Settings screen.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at smartiotinterface@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

We use AES-256 encryption for credential storage, HTTPS for all communications, and Firebase security rules to restrict data access. BLE provisioning uses encrypted payloads.

10. Your Rights (Bangladesh & General)

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: smartiotinterface@gmail.com

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
